Our Mission

Breaking Barriers in Astronomy

The Stellar Gateway believes that the wonder of the cosmos should be accessible to all. We're a passionate community of students, researchers, and educators working to democratize astronomy education and create pathways for underrepresented students to pursue careers in space science.
